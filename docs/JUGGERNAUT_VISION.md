# Juggernaut Vision

ClaimVault becomes a juggernaut by stacking three layers in order:

1. **Deterministic case engine.** The FastAPI core stays irreproachably consistent: SQLModel entities, strict workflow transitions, assistant-ready services, append-only timelines/audit logs, and deterministic exports. Every mutation is auditable, every export repeatable, and every readiness score reproducible.
2. **Guided evidence completion & extraction.** OCR/PDF/text extraction and email ingestion keep data structured. NULLA (or future assistants) turn blockers into concrete tasks, summarize timelines, suggest next steps, and classify evidence metadata while letting deterministic rules remain the source of truth.
3. **Verified vault, export, and payments.** Liquefy + DNA anchor packs the bundle, the vault/Search layer lets partners query exports, and S3/remote storage backs production runs. NULLA + Liquefy handle proof artifacts, policy/redaction, and safe restores, while DNA/paid-export credits prove every bundle to regulators and finance partners.

The roadmap follows that order: nail the core, then the guided evidence layer, and finally the verified vault/export/payment stack so ClaimVault can scale into high-trust operations without cutting corners.

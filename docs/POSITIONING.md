# Positioning

**ClaimVault** is the verified evidence and dispute operating system for operations, compliance, and back-office teams that need to resolve refunds, warranty requests, chargebacks, damaged shipments, and rental disputes with confidence.

## Outcomes we deliver
- Operators ingest messy evidence (receipts, emails, photos, notes) and immediately see the missing pieces before escalating.
- Audit teams review a hardened timeline + append-only events instead of stitching together spreadsheets or siloed folders.
- Legal/compliance squads export deterministic case packages (summary, manifest, evidence, checksums) that map directly to the dispute or refund being defended.

## Why buyers care
- You get a single source of truth for disputes instead of chasing multiple inboxes, folders, or ticketing systems.
- Evidence is hashed, traced, and attached to timelines so every export is defensible under review.
- The workflow ties readiness rules directly to actions, so teams stop guessing what is “export-ready”.

## Clear MVP boundaries
- The MVP targets case-building, evidence management, readiness scoring, audit trails, and deterministic exports.
- Partnerships or automation (Liquefy, NULLA, DNA anchoring) are explicitly future expansions, not shipped capabilities.

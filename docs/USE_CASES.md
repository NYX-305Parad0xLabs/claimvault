# Use Cases

ClaimVault is built for dispute-heavy organizations that must prove more than just a refund—it must prove the why, how, and when.

## Refunds
- Users gather receipts, merchant communications, and shipment tracking to justify a refund.
- Readiness rules verify required evidence (order references, merchant name, amount, timeline event) before export.
- Operators hand the export bundle to finance or the merchant as a defensible record.

## Warranty claims
- Technicians upload photos, work logs, and warranty terms tied to the product/order.
- The timeline captures troubleshooting steps, communications, and approvals while audit events track who reviewed/deployed each change.
- Export bundles become the record sent to claims or repair teams.

## Chargeback preparation
- Analysts consolidate transaction history, correspondence, receipts, and tracking into a case before responding to the issuer.
- Readiness blockers alert teams to missing proof (e.g., no tracking, missing receipts) so they can gather it before the deadline.
- Audited exports can be sent directly to banks, showing a locked timeline of the dispute.

## Damaged shipment claims
- Evidence items include photos, inspection notes, and carrier damage reports.
- The timeline ensures operators log each step (received, inspected, documented, approved).
- The case export provides a single zipped package that combines notes, photos, manifest, and checksums.

## Rental deposit / move-out disputes
- Property teams track move-in/out photos, inspection notes, repair estimates, and tenant communications.
- Readiness scoring ensures all documentation is captured before final settlement or export to property managers.
- Audit trail shows who inspected, approved, or escalated the case.
